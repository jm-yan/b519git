Compounds

MarinChem3D is the world's largest three-dimensional structure database of marine natural products, containing 30,117 compound molecules derived from marine organisms, with precise chemical structure, physical properties, species origin, safety, toxicity, which can be directly applied to drug virtual screening and intelligent drug design.

Targets

Drug target database is a professional database established by acquiring, processing and analyzing the three-dimensional structure and disease-related information of drug targets, and scientifically integrating and managing them.Drug target database includes not only information about drug target structure, drug binding sites and diseases, but also services in virtual screening, new target finding, drug design, disease treatment and pharmacological mechanism research.

Docking Results

The docking result database is based on the three-dimensional structure of the drug action targets in the target database, and screens the marine compound database by the computational method of molecular docking, predicts the affinity and non-bond interaction between each target and the compound, excavates the seemingly drug-forming molecules, accelerates the discovery of leading compounds with biological activity, and promotes the research and development of marine innovative drugs.

About Us

In the exploration of marine resources by human beings, more than 30,000 kinds of marine natural compounds have been discovered. It is one of the most precious treasures of our civilization. In order to turn precious knowledge into productivity and realize the dream of "Blue Pharmacy", in order to sail the marine medicine industry in China, the Pilot National Laboratory for Marine Science and Technology (Qingdao), the Qingdao Marine Biomedical Research Institute and the China Ocean University have summarized all published marine natural products by inquiring tens of thousands of scientific and technical documents. It has established a fine three-dimensional structure database containing 30117 marine natural products that can be directly invoked for artificial intelligence and virtual drug screening. The virtual drug screening is carried out in batch, fast and efficient ways with the aids from the supercomputer. On this basis, under the guidance of artificial intelligence, combined with the experimental researches of biologists and pharmacologists, we quickly discovered and developed targeted drugs for major diseases and guarded the health of all humans from our common blue treasure trove.
